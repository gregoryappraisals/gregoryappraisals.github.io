About Gregory Appraisals

Gregory Appraisals, LLC is a Georgia-based appraisal firm providing independent, well-supported valuation services for commercial, residential, and complex real estate assets. Since its founding in 2020, our firm has delivered trusted appraisal services with a focus on professionalism, compliance, and a unique perspective shaped by diverse industry experience. .

Led by Shazidy Gregory, Certified General Real Property Appraiser, the firm serves attorneys, lenders, government agencies, and private clients throughout the State of Georgia.
