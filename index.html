About Gregory Appraisals

Gregory Appraisals, LLC is a Georgia-based appraisal firm providing credible, well-supported valuation services for a wide range of property types. Founded in 2020, the firm is minority- and female-owned and is committed to professionalism, accuracy, and independence.

Led by Shazidy Gregory, Certified General Real Property Appraiser, the firm delivers appraisal reports that meet USPAP standards and are tailored to the needs of attorneys, lenders, government agencies, and private clients.
